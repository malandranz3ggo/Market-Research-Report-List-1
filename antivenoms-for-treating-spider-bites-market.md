<p><p>Antivenoms for treating spider bites are categorized into two main types: polyvalent and monovalent. Polyvalent antivenoms target multiple venom types, providing broader protection against various spider species, making them suitable for regions with diverse spider populations. In contrast, monovalent antivenoms are specific to a single venom type, offering precise treatment for bites from particular spiders. The choice between these antivenoms depends on factors such as the spider species involved, regional prevalence, and the severity of the envenomation.</p></p>
