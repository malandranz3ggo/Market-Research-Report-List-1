<p><p>The Polypropylene Industrial Tapes market encompasses two primary types: single-sided tape and double-sided tape. Single-sided tape features adhesive on one side, making it ideal for bonding, sealing, or insulating applications. It is commonly used in packaging and labeling. In contrast, double-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing for stronger adhesion between two surfaces, which is beneficial in mounting, bonding, and graphic applications. Both types are versatile and utilized across various industries for their durability and effectiveness.</p></p>
