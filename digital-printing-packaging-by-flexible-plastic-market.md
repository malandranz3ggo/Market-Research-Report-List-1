<p><ul><li>Electrophotography</li><li>Inkjet printing</li><li>Thermal transfer printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital printing packaging using flexible plastic encompasses various printing technologies: Electrophotography, inkjet printing, and thermal transfer printing. Electrophotography employs electrostatic charges to transfer dry toner onto flexible substrates, resulting in high-quality images. Inkjet printing utilizes tiny droplets of ink to create detailed and vibrant designs, making it suitable for short runs and customization. Thermal transfer printing utilizes heat to transfer ink from a ribbon onto the plastic, providing durability and resistance to fading. Each technology offers unique advantages for packaging solutions.</p></p>
