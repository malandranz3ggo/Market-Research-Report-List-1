<p><p>The Micro Water Spraying Fertilizer Integrated Machine market is categorized into two primary types: Film Irrigation and Under Film Irrigation. Film Irrigation involves applying water and fertilizers directly onto the soil surface through a layer of plastic film, enhancing moisture retention and nutrient absorption. In contrast, Under Film Irrigation places the delivery system beneath the plastic cover, allowing for efficient water management while reducing evaporation. Both methods aim to optimize crop yields and enhance resource efficiency in agriculture.</p></p>
