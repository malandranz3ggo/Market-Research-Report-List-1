<p><p>The Point of Sale (POS) terminal battery market features three main types: Lead-Acid batteries, Li-Ion batteries, and Ni-Cd batteries. Lead-Acid batteries are robust and cost-effective, making them suitable for stationary applications. Li-Ion batteries offer higher energy density, longer life cycles, and lighter weight, making them ideal for portable POS devices. Ni-Cd batteries are known for their durability and ability to perform well in extreme temperatures, though they have been largely replaced by more efficient technologies.</p></p>
