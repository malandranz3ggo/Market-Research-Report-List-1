<p><ul><li>Leafing Aluminum Pigment Powder</li><li>Non-leafing Aluminum Pigment Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum pigment powder market comprises two primary types: leafing and non-leafing aluminum pigment powders. Leafing aluminum pigments float on the surface of coatings, creating a reflective, metallic finish that enhances visual appeal and glossiness. In contrast, non-leafing aluminum pigments are dispersed throughout the coating material, providing a consistent, pearlescent effect without a distinct surface layer. These distinct properties cater to varying applications in paints, coatings, plastics, and cosmetics, influencing aesthetic qualities and performance characteristics in end products.</p></p>
