<p><p>The hydraulic industrial brake market is primarily segmented into three types: disc brakes, drum brakes, and others. Disc brakes use a metal rotor and offer improved heat dissipation and stopping power, making them popular in high-performance applications. Drum brakes consist of a drum and brake shoes, providing reliable performance in various conditions, often at a lower cost. The "others" category includes specialized braking systems, such as hydraulic actuated or combined systems, catering to specific industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
