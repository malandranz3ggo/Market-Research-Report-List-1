<p><p>The flea control market is categorized into three main types: sprays, powders, and baits. Sprays are liquid solutions that target fleas on contact, providing rapid action and often featuring residual benefits. Powders are granular formulations that can be dusted onto carpets and surfaces, offering long-lasting protection as they cling to fibers. Baits attract fleas and contain insecticides that eliminate them upon consumption. Each type caters to different user preferences and application methods, ensuring effective flea management in various environments.</p></p>
