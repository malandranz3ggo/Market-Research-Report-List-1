Single-channel pipettes are designed for precise liquid handling in individual samples, offering high accuracy for tasks such as PCR applications. In contrast, multi-channel pipettes allow simultaneous handling of multiple samples, significantly increasing throughput in high-volume procedures, such as ELISA assays or plate preparation. Both types enhance laboratory efficiency and precision, catering to diverse research and clinical needs in various scientific fields.</p></p>
