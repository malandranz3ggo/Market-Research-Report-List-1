<p><p>The fluorinated resins market includes several key types, each with distinct properties and applications. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) is renowned for its high-temperature resistance and non-stick characteristics. PCTFE (polychlorotrifluoroethylene) offers excellent chemical resistance and low permeability. PVDF (polyvinylidene fluoride) showcases strong mechanical properties and UV resistance. ETFE (ethylene tetrafluoroethylene) is valued for its durability and electrical insulation. ECTFE (ethylene-chlorotrifluoroethylene) combines the benefits of PTFE and PVDF, while "Others" encompasses additional specialized fluorinated resins used in niche applications across various industries.</p></p>
