<p><p>The electric automotive battery market comprises various types, primarily lithium-ion (Li-ion), nickel-metal hydride (NiMH), and lead-acid batteries. Li-ion batteries dominate due to their high energy density, longer life, and lighter weight, making them ideal for modern electric vehicles. NiMH batteries, used in hybrid vehicles, offer good performance but are heavier and less efficient than Li-ion.
