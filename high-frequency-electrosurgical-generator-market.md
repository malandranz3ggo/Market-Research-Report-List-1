<p><strong>The High Frequency Electrosurgical Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Circuit</li><li>Bipolar Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Frequency Electrosurgical Generator market comprises two main types: monopolar and bipolar circuits. Monopolar circuits utilize a single active electrode to deliver high-frequency electrical currents to a targeted tissue area, with a grounding pad completing the circuit. This method is effective for cutting and coagulating tissue. Conversely, bipolar circuits involve two electrodes, allowing current to flow between them, which enhances precision and minimizes thermal spread. Both types are essential in various surgical procedures, enhancing safety and efficacy.</p></p>
