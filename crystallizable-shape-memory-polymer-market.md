<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crystallizable Shape Memory Polymers (CSMPs) are categorized into two types based on their thermal behavior and applications. Type I CSMPs exhibit a single transition temperature, where they can recover their original shape upon heating. They are primarily used in applications requiring simple shape recovery. Type II CSMPs, on the other hand, feature multiple transition temperatures, allowing for more complex shape recovery and programmable behavior. This versatility makes them suitable for advanced applications in aerospace, biomedical, and smart materials sectors.</p></p>
