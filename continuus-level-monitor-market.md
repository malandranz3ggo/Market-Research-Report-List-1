<p><p>Continuus Level Monitors are categorized into various market types based on their measurement principles. Differential pressure sensors detect pressure changes to assess fluid levels. Guided wave radar uses microwave pulses along a probe, providing precise measurements. Non-contact radar employs microwaves from a distance for level detection without direct contact. Ultrasonic monitors utilize sound waves to gauge levels. Capacitance sensors measure changes in capacitance due to the presence of materials. Other market types may include advanced technologies like laser-based systems or optical sensors.</p></p>
