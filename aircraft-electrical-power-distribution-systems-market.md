<p><strong>The Aircraft Electrical Power Distribution Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEPDS (Primary Electrical Power Distribution System)</li><li>SPDS (Secondary Power Distribution System)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Electrical Power Distribution Systems market encompasses two main types: the Primary Electrical Power Distribution System (PEPDS) and the Secondary Power Distribution System (SPDS). PEPDS is responsible for the initial distribution of electrical power generated by the aircraft's engines or auxiliary power units, ensuring essential systems receive power. In contrast, SPDS manages the distribution of power to various subsystems and components, optimizing efficiency and redundancy. Together, these systems ensure reliable electrical supply throughout the aircraft.</p></p>
